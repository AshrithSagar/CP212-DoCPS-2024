\section*{Question 2}

What does AA mean in part number: nRF52833-QIAA.\@

\subsection*{Solution}

The \texttt{AA} in the part number denotes the \underline{function variant}, corresponding to a flash memory of size 512 kB and RAM of size 128 kB.
